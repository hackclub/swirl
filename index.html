Who?

Swirl is open to all Hack Clubbers! All teenagers 18 and under, anywhere in the world can participate!


What?

Swirl is the next step after the completion of Boba Drops! It's more independent than Boba Drops, as it is all about you making your own unique website!
It's your time to shine and push yourself further than ever before — uniqueness is encouraged!
You will get a food grant between $5 and $9 by shipping a project, dependent on how many features you add!
See the requirements page for more information.


How?


Cool features can be found all over the internet, it is encouraged for you to do research on specific attributes you may want to add, just remember to not copy-paste templates, and don't claim other's code as your own!
There is a guide dedicated to Swirl and it is recommended that you check it out!
